# Tribute-Page Tribute Page to Sir Daniel of Belmont

Sir Daniel Marshall of Belmont

The Conservation Coder

Sir Daniel Marshall, pictured above, is an Aonic Knight from Teo.

Here's a timeline of Sir Daniel's life:

  • 1312 - Born in Arelon
  • 1313 - Kidnapped by Pygmies and taken to Fjordell
  • 1313 - Trained day and night to become a world famous Pygmy gourmet chef
  • 1332 - Escaped certain doom at the hands of the Mad Marzipan Mincer and forever earned the respect of the Pygmy chief
  • 1333 - Was allowed to journey home by the Pygmies
  • 1333 - Journeyed home, defeating the Chocolate Bandit on Candy Crush Lane along the way
  • 1337 - Earned Knighthood by saving Teo from the ravenous maw of the Glutton Gang
  • 1348 - Retired due to hunger

"Sir Daniel's life was a hectic concoction of horrible violence and damned good food."

- Gumbo, Chief of the United Federation of Pygmy Chefs
